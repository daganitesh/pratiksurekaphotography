Pratik Sureka

Pratik Sureka a self-taught photographer who opted to forego of his career as CA CS because of his passion for photography.

>

He is the official photographer for events like TEDx Dharavi, IIT MOODi, IIT techfest.



His passion for wedding photography stems from the deep fascination by capturing two people coming together as one, their emotions and connection.



Shooting weddings for Pratik has always meant more than just his work. To capture the emotions of the people, the friends and families and immortalising the special day of the bride and groom is what defines his work. He believes that the role of a photographer in weddings is primarily the most important one for people will forget the days but the memories will stay forever.



He has also been working as an assistant to Ms Victoria Krundysheva, being a part of such shoots as Milind Soman and Ankita Konwar’s campaign for Kompanero, editorial shoot with Nushrat Bharucha for Hello magazine, Isha Ambanis pre-wedding functions coverage and Manish Malhotra’s shoots as well as many more.
